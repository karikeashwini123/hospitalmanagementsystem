    Helping patients and their families make an informed decision

    At RVM Hospitals, we believe that consumers should know, long before they get their bill, what a hospital will charge for the services provided. As part of our effort to increase price transparency, Apollo Hospitals introduced Apollo Surgery, a surgical package program with assured pricing.


    The RVM Surgery program, backed by the trust built over three decades, is committed to providing guaranteed (assured) price transparency for any surgery to help patients and their families make the most informed decision for their healthcare needs. Using the most innovative technology and surgical procedures, specialists, nurses and the staff at Apollo Hospitals work together closely to ensure that surgeries are handled with the utmost professionalism, always keeping in mind the well-being and comfortable recovery of a patient.
